Services

At "truecatspr," we offer customized promotion packages tailored to your individual needs. Our approach starts with an in-depth consultation where we define your goals and develop an appropriate strategy.

Our Process

Before we start the promotion, we create a detailed list of materials that outlines exactly what content you need to produce as an artist. This includes:

  • Biographies and press texts
  • High-quality photos and artworks
  • Music files in different formats
  • Videos and teasers


We need the materials at least 6 weeks before the planned release date to adjust them for different platforms and schedule the necessary posts. This ensures that your release is professional and timely.
