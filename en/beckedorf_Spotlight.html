Judith Beckedorf

Acoustic Guitar, Singer/Songwriter

"The warm tones of her guitar are like a blanket that gently envelops her soft voice. Fingerstyle and singer/songwriter become one."

Judith Beckedorf discovered her passion for music at the age of 14 and quickly established herself as a talented solo performer. Her dedication led her to study acoustic guitar in depth, making Dresden her home base while drawing inspiration from the vibrant music scene in Nashville, Tennessee.

On her album “Behind The Blue Sea”, introspective lyrics blend with folky melodies, snappy grooves, and graceful ballads. Her music explores the intricacies of life—from the beauty of fleeting moments to the bittersweet nature of time, as captured in the title track “Don’t Waste Your Time.”

Beyond her solo work, Judith is a key member of the progressive folk/americana trio “Standard Crow Behavior,” where she masterfully combines guitar, banjo, and mandolin with intricate vocal harmonies. In Nashville, she founded the “International Guitar Nights” concert series and co-launched “MusiSHEans,” a network project dedicated to promoting female musicians.

Currently, Judith is venturing into new musical territories with innovative projects that push the boundaries of traditional folk music. Looking ahead, she plans to expand her repertoire and collaborate with a diverse array of artists, promising an exciting evolution in her sound and creative expression.
