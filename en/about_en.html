About "truecatspr"

"truecatspr" is an independent promo label that helps musicians advance their careers by offering competent advice. A small team that combines its experience as musicians and entrepreneurs to make it easier for artists to break into the music industry.

Our Goal

At "truecatspr," we pursue a clear vision: We aim to promote a vibrant, authentic, and diverse music landscape where artists can unleash their creative freedom while receiving the recognition they deserve.

We believe that the music world thrives on fresh, untainted voices. Our goal is to discover, support, and place these voices in the right spots. We focus on authenticity and quality—because only then can we offer true value to both the artists and their audiences.

We see ourselves as bridges between artists and their audiences. Through targeted promotion and strategic positioning, we ensure that new music reaches the right people. We use modern marketing channels and proven methods to maximize reach.

Our approach is holistic: We don’t just want to help artists advance their careers but also provide true value to their audience. By bringing authentic music to the right places, we create a connection between artists and their audience based on mutual understanding and excitement.

Our commitment doesn’t end with the release of a song or album. We accompany our artists in the long run and help them establish themselves in the music industry. We focus on sustainable strategies that enable not only short-term success but also long-term growth.

How We Work

At "truecatspr," we rely on a transparent, structured, and artist-friendly approach. Our goal is to take the burden off musicians so they can focus entirely on their creative work. Here’s how we achieve this:

  • Individual Consultation: In an in-depth consultation, we analyze your current situation and develop a tailor-made strategy.
  • Professional Promotion: We take care of all promotion for you—from social media to playlist pitching.
  • Creative Freedom: We create space for your creative work by taking over time-consuming self-marketing tasks.
  • Transparent Communication: You are always kept informed about all the steps and successes of our work.
  • Long-term Partnership: We support you in the long run and help you establish yourself sustainably in the music industry.
